Compression

Image compression is the process of reducing the number of pixels in an image, while trying to retain the necessary information to recreate the image. The main purpose of image compression is to reduce the amount of data that needs to be stored or transmitted. Image compression is used in many applications, including digital photography, video conferencing, video streaming, and video editing.
